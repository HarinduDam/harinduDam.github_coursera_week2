It studies matter, forces and their effects. The word physics comes from the Greek word ἡ φύσις, meaning "nature". Physics can also be defined as "that department of knowledge which relates to the order of nature, or, in other words, to the regular succession of events". Astronomy, a part of physics, is the oldest natural science.

Physics
